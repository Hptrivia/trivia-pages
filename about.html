About Trivia Gauntlet

Trivia Gauntlet is a free collection of themed trivia quizzes for fans who like putting their knowledge to the test. Pick a topic you love — a show, a film, a game — answer a set of multiple-choice questions, and see your score in a couple of minutes. There is nothing to install and no account to make.

How it works

Every quiz lives on its own page with all of its questions laid out in front of you. Choose an answer to find out straight away whether you got it right, watch your running total climb, and see a final score once you reach the end. If you want another go, you can reset and replay any quiz as many times as you like.

What you'll find here

The quizzes lean toward entertainment and fandom — popular series, movies, anime, and video games — with questions written to be approachable for newcomers while still holding a few surprises for people who know a subject inside out. Each topic comes with a short write-up so you know what the quiz covers before you dive in.

Growing over time

New quiz topics are added regularly, and older ones are revisited and refined. If there is a subject you would like to see covered, suggestions are always welcome.
