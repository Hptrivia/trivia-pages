About this Pokémon quiz

A good Pokémon quiz can stretch from the original Kanto region to the newest games — evolutions, abilities, regions, rivals, and the creatures that became mascots for the whole series. It works because players tend to remember both the big milestones and oddly specific facts about who evolves from what.

This quiz includes 20 multiple-choice questions covering a range of difficulty. Every question is shown on the page, so you can work through them at your own pace, pick an answer to see whether it was correct, and check your final score at the end. There is no time limit and you can replay it as often as you like.
