The Wire Trivia Quiz

The Wire is HBO's sprawling Baltimore crime drama that examines the city one institution at a time — the drug trade, the police, the docks, City Hall, the schools, and the newspaper. Across five seasons it follows detective Jimmy McNulty and a struggling police unit as they chase the Barksdale and Stanfield organisations, while showing how the same broken systems trap cops and corner kids alike.
